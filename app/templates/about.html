740-IIMA

Dharshini Ilango

ISB

Dharshini, who holds a degree in Electronics and Electrical Engineering, launched her career as the Marketing and Strategic Head for a real estate firm in Tamil Nadu, where she collaborated closely with senior government officials to develop energy infrastructure throughout the region.
